Accessible research papers for everyone.

Student's Open-Access Research E-Directory (SOARED) collect research works by high school student that touch the topics of humanities, sciences, technology, engineering, and math.

Conducting research and writing a scientific journal can show your academic ability and help your college application to stand out.

Showcasing your work can help and inspire other students to start their own research.
